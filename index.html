About hauto.

Since 2020, hauto. has been transforming homes with smart automation and practical electrical solutions. From intelligent lighting and climate control to voice-activated systems, we design and install technology that adapts to the way you live. In addition to smart home solutions, we provide non-notifiable electrical work — from lighting and sockets to general home upgrades — delivering safe, efficient, and tailored installations. Our mission is to make homes smarter, simpler, and more connected, combining innovative technology with practical electrical expertise. hauto. — home automation, done right.
