Intelligent Smart Blind Installations

Transform your living environment with automated shading solutions. At hauto., we specialise in the installation and integration of smart blinds that adapt to your lifestyle, offering the perfect balance of natural light, privacy, and convenience.

The Power of Automated Shading

Smart blinds are more than just a luxury; they are a functional upgrade to your home's ecosystem. By automating your window treatments, you can schedule your home to wake up with the sun or protect your furniture from UV damage while you're away at work.

Benefits of Smart Blinds

Energy Efficiency: Automatically close your blinds during the hottest part of the day to keep your home cool, or open them in winter to harvest natural solar heat, significantly reducing HVAC costs.

Enhanced Privacy & Security: Set "Away Mode" schedules that make your home look occupied by moving the blinds at different intervals throughout the day, even when you are on holiday.

Voice & App Control: Adjust hard-to-reach windows with a simple voice command or via your smartphone. No more manual cords—keeping your home safer for children and pets.

Seamless Integration: Link your shading with your smart lighting. As the sun sets and your blinds close, your indoor lights can gently dim up to create the perfect evening ambiance.

Our team provides a professional, end-to-end service from technical consultation to final integration. We ensure your motors are quiet, your alignment is precise, and your controls are intuitively mapped to your existing smart home platform.
