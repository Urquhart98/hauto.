Immersive Multi-Room Sound Systems

Experience high-fidelity audio throughout your home with professionally installed sound systems. At hauto., we design and install bespoke audio solutions that blend seamlessly into your interior, from discreet ceiling speakers to powerful home cinema setups.

The Ultimate Audio Experience

Music should be heard, not seen. We focus on integrating world-class audio hardware into your home's architecture, allowing you to stream your favourite playlists, podcasts, or films in any room with total control at your fingertips.

Benefits of Integrated Audio

Multi-Room Control: Play different music in every room or sync your entire home for a "party mode" experience, all controlled via a single app or voice command.

Invisible Hardware: We specialise in in-ceiling and in-wall speaker installations, providing incredible sound quality without the clutter of traditional floor-standing units and visible cables.

Cinema Quality at Home: Transform your living space with surround sound configurations (5.1, 7.1, or Dolby Atmos) that put you right in the middle of the action.

Smart Scene Integration: Link your audio with your home automation. Have your morning playlist start gradually with your smart blinds, or ensure the music fades out automatically when the doorbell rings.

Why Choose hauto.

We go beyond just plugging in speakers. Our team handles the complex cabling, network optimisation, and acoustic calibration required to ensure your sound system performs perfectly. We bridge the gap between high-end audio and smart home convenience.
